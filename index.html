ABOUT US

Eclipse Analytics was founded in xxx to provide a forecast model to help racing and breeding organisations around the world to better understand the genetic potential of their horses by employing a unique algorithm that examines all aspects of a horses pedigree and some relevant environmental factors. Taking account of how racing is organised in different parts of the world, the algorithm generates unique and insightful analysis for broodmares, young stock and stallions.
